Campus Navigation: Scavenger Hunt Game

JS/TS, Tailwind CSS, React.js, PostgreSQL | CEN4033 - Secure Software Engineering | 01/2025 - 04/2025

Iterating upon an assistive campus navigation website from another student research team, we developed a scavenger hunt game centered around players searching for NFC tags, discovering the tags, and scanning them. The map was used to display the range of area that the NFC tag could be located in. Players could then answer educational trivia questions to make the search range smaller, honing in on the NFC tag location. Finally, when the player scans the NFC tag, they can enter their information into a leaderboard for speed of finding the tag. The tag was password protected, requiring finding the tag to know what password to put in to enter a record.
